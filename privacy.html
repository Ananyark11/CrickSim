Privacy Policy

Last updated 8 July 2026

CrickSim is built to collect as little as possible. There are no accounts, and we do not store your personal data on any server. This page explains what that means in practice.

No accounts, no sign-up

You can play CrickSim without registering. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information to use the game.

Data stored on your own device

Your season history, saved games, and preferences are stored in your browser's local storage, on your device only. This information never leaves your device and we cannot see or access it. You can erase it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for CrickSim.

No server-side personal data

CrickSim is a static website. We (the operators) do not run a database of users and do not collect or retain personal information about you on our servers.

Advertising and cookies

The site may display advertising provided by third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdSense or AdMob). To serve and measure ads, these networks may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies, and may collect information such as your approximate location, device type, and interactions with ads. This may include personalised advertising based on your activity across sites and apps.

  • We do not control the data these third parties collect; it is governed by their own policies.
  • You can learn how Google uses advertising data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • You can manage or opt out of personalised ads at adssettings.google.com.
  • For visitors in the EU, UK, and other regions requiring it, advertising is subject to your consent.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics to understand overall usage (such as how many people visit). Where used, this is not tied to your identity.

Children

CrickSim is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the site, contact us and we will address it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the EU/UK GDPR and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because we hold no personal data about you on our servers, most requests concern (a) data stored locally on your device, which you control and can clear yourself, and (b) advertising personalisation, which you manage through the ad settings linked above. For any question, contact us.

Data security

The site is served over HTTPS. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is no such data for us to lose.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.
