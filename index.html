I

The Auction

Fifty player-season lots under the lights — Kohli ’16 and Kohli ’24 are different cards with different ratings. Nine AI franchises bid against you, each with its own money, needs and temperament. Your purse: ₹50cr to unlimited. Your call.

II

The Squad

Fifteen names on your sheet, eleven shirts to fill. Max four overseas. One keeper, real bowling depth, someone for the death overs. The selection screen is where auctions are truly won or lost.

III

The Season

Fourteen league games, then the playoffs. The simulation is phase-based and merciless — your weakest link sets your ceiling. A batting paradise with no death bowler does not go 16‑0. Nothing ever has.
